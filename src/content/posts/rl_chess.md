Started out with the [Intro to TF-Agents lecture](https://youtu.be/U7g7-Jzj9qo); from this I first saw that the way RL generally works is that we have an **environment** and an **agent**. Each step has three things that happen: 1. the environment sends an *observation* to the agent, 2. the agent sends back an *action*, 3. the environment gives a *reward*.
